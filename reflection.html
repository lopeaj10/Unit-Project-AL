Reflection By Alex Lopez

I wasn't anticipating it, but I came across a lot more difficulties than I was expecting. At first, working through GitHub and familiarizing myself with how it works went well, alongside the videos, I was having fine time working through everything and making sure it was supposed to go as intended. At first, I thought everything was fine, but then my attached image wasn't showing up. I was really confused about that, I thought that there shouldn't be anything wrong with it, so I went over the image again and again, making sure it wasn't a bad file type or something, making sure I wrote the file name correctly, and just changing things up and redoing them again and again while constantly visiting updating previews of my page. Nothing seemed to work, what's worse is that I would try to right click the option "load image" or "open image in new tab" and it just told me the image didn't exist, which really made me annoyed. Finally, I figured out that the issue must have had something to do with how I was writing the tag, so I redid it, and finally, it worked. My celebration wasn't for long though, because while everything looked fine now, the clickable hyper-link wasn't working, which really annoyed me because if I copy and paste the same link into my search bar, it works just fine! I looked at the url, it was fine, I looked at the href tag, it was correct too, for a while I simply had no idea why this link wasn't working because otherwise it was perfectly fine. I kept playing around with it for a while before I realized I had placed one part of the tag incorrectly, finally I was able to fix it. It took a ton of trial and error and being so specific about writing things in a certain way but finally I got it to work which I feel proud of being able to do after trying it for a while.
